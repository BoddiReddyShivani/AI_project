Safety Tips

These are practical checks every applicant should follow before sharing documents or making payments.

Never pay to get hired.
Registration, interview, security, training, and document fees are common scam tactics.
Verify the domain.
Official recruiters usually contact you from the company domain, not from random free email services.
Protect sensitive data.
Do not share OTP, UPI PIN, CVV, or bank details during early recruitment stages.
